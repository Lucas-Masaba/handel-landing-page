## SEO Writing Best Practices

### 1. Title Tags (55-60 characters)
**Good:** "International Trade Law Services in Uganda | Handel Advocates"
**Bad:** "Trade Law"

### 2. Meta Descriptions (150-160 characters)
**Good:** "Expert international trade law services for Uganda businesses. EAC, COMESA compliance, customs law, trade policy advisory. Contact Handel Advocates."
**Bad:** "We offer legal services."

### 3. Headings Structure
```
H1: Main title (once per page, include primary keyword)
H2: Main sections (include variations of keyword)
H3: Subsections (include long-tail keywords)
H4: Minor sections
```

### 4. Keyword Usage
- **Title:** Include primary keyword
- **First 100 words:** Use primary keyword
- **Headings:** Use keyword variations
- **Throughout:** Natural placement (1-2% density)
- **Alt tags:** Describe images with keywords when relevant

### 5. Internal Linking
- Link to related services
- Link to relevant blog posts
- Use descriptive anchor text
- Minimum 3-5 internal links per page

### 6. External Linking
- Link to authoritative sources
- Reference laws and regulations
- Cite statistics with sources
- Link to government websites

### 7. Content Length
- **Homepage:** 1,000-1,500 words
- **Service pages:** 1,500-2,000 words
- **Blog posts:** 1,800-2,500 words
- **Practice areas:** 1,000-1,500 words

### 8. Readability
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Bullet points and lists
- Subheadings every 200-300 words
- Simple language (aim for 8th-grade reading level)
- Active voice

## Remember:

**Quality > Quantity** - One excellent 2,500-word post is better than five 300-word posts.

**Consistency is Key** - Better to publish 2 posts/month consistently than 8 posts one month and none the next.

**Answer Real Questions** - Write content your clients actually need, not just for keywords.

**E-A-T Matters** - Demonstrate Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness in every piece.

**Update Regularly** - Keep content fresh and current.
